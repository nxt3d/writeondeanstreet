A brief history of Write on Dean Street

  • 2015

    Industrious Brooklyn

    I was thrilled to hear about a coworking space opening up in my neighborhood. At first I thought I would only rent a desk, but there was a problem. In order to do my work as a writer and editor, I need a phone free workspace, so I decided to rent a whole office and start a space for writers like me who need a quiet professional phone free place to work.

  • February 2016

    Write on Dean Street

    Write on Dean Street was opened and very quickly we had a group of writers working away together in the space.

  • October 2016

    Publications

    Writer on Dean Street memebrs have been publishing! Greg Milner's book Pinpoint was released.

  • March 2017

    Industous Manhattan

    Industious Manhattan opens, giving our memebrs 24/7 access to common work space at Union Square in Manhattan.
